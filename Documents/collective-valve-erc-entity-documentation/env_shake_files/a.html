    Friday November 5, 2004

    Source SDK Tools Available Now
    [Fri Nov 05, 2004 / 02:46pm PST] Valve Source SDK - comments (48)

    Valve has released the Source Software Development Kit (SDK) Tools via Steam. A precursor to the release of the full SDK, which will be released shortly after Half-Life 2 is made available, the Source SDK Tools release offers a comprehensive toolset for starting production on Source-based MODs, including Hammer, XSI EXP for Half-Life 2, compiling tools, the Source Model Viewer, documentation on programming, modeling, building materials, and more. For more information, please visit http://www.valve-erc.com/srcsdk/.
    [Source / documentation]

    Tuesday October 12, 2004

    Half Life: Visitors Released
    [Tue Oct 12, 2004 / 07:46am PDT] Kevin 'spect3r' Foster - comments (8)


    Although this mod was released almost a month ago, I thought I would bring it to your attention.

      Dr. Alan Green published his book 'Black Mesa Background' in August 2003, just one year ago. This book is about his one-week job in the Black Mesa Research Facility as a state-controlled safety evaluator within a fact-finding commission to carry out a safety report. But the job changed his life completely; and after the publication of his book his life was changed again.


    HL:V uses all of the standard half life models, but has 2 new weapons; a pipe and a sniper rifle. There are also a few other new items in the game. I will leave it up to you to play it through. I will say that it is enjoyable, memorable and generally well done and fun to play.

    Head over to their website for downloads!

    The mod also comes in steam format. Download size is just over 40mb.
